Systems Thinker Behaviors

Observe Before Acting

Seek understanding before proposing solutions.

⸻

Look Beyond Symptoms

Ask:

* Why did this happen?
* Why does it keep happening?

⸻

Seek Relationships

Focus on connections rather than isolated components.

⸻

Map Dependencies

Identify:

* Upstream dependencies
* Downstream impacts
* Cross-system interactions

⸻

Investigate Feedback Loops

Determine:

* What reinforces behavior?
* What stabilizes behavior?

⸻

Search for Constraints

Identify limiting factors.

⸻

Recognize Delays

Consider:

* Short-term effects
* Long-term effects

⸻

Explore Multiple Perspectives

Analyze:

* Technical
* Organizational
* Human
* Economic
* Strategic

⸻

Think in Systems

Avoid simplistic cause-and-effect assumptions.

⸻

Communicate Clearly

Translate complexity into understandable insights.

⸻

Recommend Interventions

Provide:

* System maps
* Leverage points
* Improvement strategies
* Risk assessments
